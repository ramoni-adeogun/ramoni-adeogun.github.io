Ramoni Adeogun, PhD, SMIEEE


I am an Assistant Professor in Machine Learning for Beyond 5G Communications with the Wireless Communication Networks section of the Department of Electronic Systems of Aalborg University, Denmark. My research interests lie in the areas of Bayesian and statistical signal processing, machine learning, Artifical Inteligence and their application to radio resource management, interference coordination, channel prediction and modelling.

Between 2017 and 2021, I was a Postdoctoral Research Fellow with the same department where I worked on multi-link radio channel modelling using propagation graphs and later on radio design for B5G Wireless Isochronous Real Time (WIRT) systems.

Since January 2020, I have also been working in tight collaboration with Nokia Bell Labs Aalborg as an External Research Engineer majorly on a project on design of 6G wireless interface using artificial inteligence.

I received the B.Eng. degree (with first-class honors) in Electrical and Computer engineering from the Federal University of Technology Minna, Minna, Nigeria, in 2007 and a Ph.D. degree in Electronics and Computer Systems Engineering (with specialization in Communication and Signal Processing) from Victoria University of Wellington, Wellington, New Zealand in 2015.

Between 2008 and 2009, I was with the Information and Communication Technology Directorate, University of Jos, Jos, Nigeria. In 2009, I worked briefly as a Telecommunications Engineer with Odua Telecoms Ltd., Ibadan, Nigeria. Between 2010 and 2016, I worked with Engineering and Space Science (ESS) Division of the National Space Research and Development Agency (NASRDA), Abuja, Nigeria as a Principal Engineer. I also held a one-year postdoctoral research fellow at the University of Cape Town in 2016.

I serve as a reviewer for IEEE Transactions on Vehicular Technology, IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing, IEEE Signal Processing Letters, EURASIP Journal on Wireless Communications and Networking, and other international journals and conferences. I have served as Technical Programme Committee (TPC) member for several international conferences. I am a senior member of Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers (IEEE) and a member of International Association of Engineers (IAENG) and a registered engineer with the Council for the Regulation of Engineering in Nigeria (COREN).
